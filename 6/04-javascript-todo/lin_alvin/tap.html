Tap
Tap is a browser-based game made by me entirely with JavaScript and SVG. To play, click the dot when it is on top of its corresponding color. The game will end when you click a dot on the wrong color or if you let a dot reach the other side. Currently, Tap is only supported by Google Chrome and Chromium.
