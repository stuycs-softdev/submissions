This is How I Develop

The world is more focused through a camera lens.

Introduction

I love taking photos, ever since I bought a real-life film camera a few years ago--none of that hipster Polaroid crap (though I have one of those, too). There's something about capuring an image on a simple piece of photo paper that just intrigues me. People used to--and still do, in fact--believe that a photo captures your 'soul'. I guess that could be true...

Passion

Everyone has their own passion; one could have a passion for food (I just so happen to be watching "Chopped" right now, so....). Photography isn't necessarily a passion of mine--I'm merely a non-Starbucks-loving white girl who loves Instagram WAY too much and pretends to be cool with her film camera and beanies :)
